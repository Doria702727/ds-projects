Study Permit Approval Rate by DLI as of October 31, 2025

Data Scope and Methodology

Data Scope

  • Source: Data is based on an IRCC ATIP report on Study Permit application outcomes by Designated Learning Institution (DLI).
  • Time period: The data covers applications processed up to October 31, 2025.
  • Application type: The statistics include Study Permit applications only. Study Permit Extension outcomes are excluded.

Meaningful Sample

  • To improve interpretability, this visualization includes only DLIs with a 2025 Study Permit Total Processed Number greater than 1,000.

Approval Rate

  • Approval rates are displayed exactly as reported in the ATIP report.

Disclaimer

  • This visualization is based on a large ATIP dataset and OCR-assisted extraction. While targeted QA checks were performed, minor transcription or processing discrepancies may remain.
